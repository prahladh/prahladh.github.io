Research Interests

High-fidelity direct numerical simulations (DNS) of complex flows.
Supersonic/ hypersonic transition to turbulence, roughness effects.
Global & local stability analysis for complex flows.
Low-speed jets in crossflow.
Dynamic Mode Decomposition of fluid flows.
Moving Boundary Methods using Arbitrary Lagrangian-Eulerian (ALE) technique.
